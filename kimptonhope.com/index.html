The story of us



We met back in 2017 very romantically online (big props to Tom and other Tom for moving ​away and leaving Ed all alone, leading him to online dating). Our first few conversations should ​have been a red flag for Jessie; Ed described his event for the day as cooking a pig on a spit with ​his friends, jokingly adding that he hoped Jessie wasn’t a vegetarian. Awkward. Luckily, Jessie ​was blinded by how cute he looked in his photos.


Over the past seven years, we have traveled together, spent endless hours gossiping, driven on ​some questionable roads, embarked on new careers and started new jobs (mostly Ed), skated, ​ridden bikes, bought a home, went to the Eras Tour (!!!), and most importantly, relentlessly ​bullied each other (mostly Jessie).


They say friends are the family you choose, and how lucky are we to have picked up each one of ​you along the way to be a part of our huge dysfunctional family. We’re so excited to be ​embarking on this new journey together and so happy you will be there with us on our wedding ​day.
